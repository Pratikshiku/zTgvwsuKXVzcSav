## Campus Shop Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/4fca4140d65040519e7501e0660d7ab6/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (any of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Functional Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, merchants, administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Announcement Information
5. Merchants
6. Personal Center
7. Delivery Address
8. Product Favorites
9. Product Orders
10. Shopping Cart
11. Change Password
12. Personal Information
13. Announcement Information Management
14. Product Information
15. Product Reviews
16. Product Favorites
17. Product Orders


**Merchant Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Announcement Information Management
6. Product Management
7. Product Review Management
8. Product Order Management



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Change Password
3. Personal Information
4. Announcement Type Management
5. Merchant Credit Type Management
6. Product Type Management
7. Announcement Information Management
8. Merchant Management
9. Product Management
10. Product Review Management
11. Product Favorite Management
12. Product Order Management
13. User Management
14. Carousel Management
